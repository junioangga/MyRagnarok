Story

Ragnarok is a story of valhalla and earth. Gods are conquering humans and make them suffer. Humans that finally tired of being a lesser existence fight back. Is the one female god, Valkyrie, that with her devotion towards human, unleashed limits of some humans. Those chosen by the Valkyrie gone through a rough proccess called "Rebirth." Many fails but those who survived granted with an unlimited potential, even may surpassed the gods. Thus, Ragnarok begins.

Characters

Tono
A young lad dedicated himself to be a ranger. Lost both of his parents to Ragnarok, this man hates the war. However, the more he deny it, the more it is clear. Humans need his help.

Bimo
A rune that is trying to stop ragnarok. He is a leader of a guild called "Shining Force," a small but solid guild filled with passionate people. Hearing about a skilled ranger named Tono, he traveled all the way to aldebaran to recruit him.

Siti
Tono's childhood friend. She trained to be an acolyte since a little child to aid Tono since she believed that Tono will eventually join the Ragnarok. Having no healing gifts and tossed around by many priest associations, Tono took her and made her one of the most excellent ranger in Rune Midgar.
